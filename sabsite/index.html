OUR MISSION is to bring together everyone with a love for Super Smash Brothers and to create a long-lasting Smash community here at Cal. Smash at Berkeley is the premiere club that provides a place to learn, grow, and inspire a community of smashers here at the world's No. 1 public university. Through workshops, smashfests, crew battles, and tournaments, we aim to create a diverse, but unified group of people with a love for the Super Smash Brother series. Welcome to Bear Territory.
